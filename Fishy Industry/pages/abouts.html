About Me

My name is Stanley Lam and I am a rising Junior at Lowell. I am currently an intern at Code Tenderloin learning about web development and design. This website is my final project for the program. I watched the Seaspiracy documentary a few weeks ago and I was astonished by the corrupted fishing industry and the immense amount of damage the industry was causing and has caused. I was shocked about the secrets of the fishing industry and what our governments were hiding. I was moved by the film so I decided that my final project for my internship was to create an informative website about the documentary and let others learn and discover the secrets that the fishing industry was hiding.

About Seaspiracy

Seaspiracy is a 2021 documentary film about the environmental impact of fishing. It is directed by and starring Ali Tabrize, a british filmmaker. The documentary examines various human impacts on marine life and advocates for ending fish consumption. To learn more about Seaspiracy or to support them visit their Website!
